# Quick Wins Analysis - East Coast Commune

**Date:** 2025-12-10
**Source:** December 3, 2025 discovery call + project brief

---

## Overview

**Top Priority:** Unified partnership dashboard for real-time event/financial/schedule visibility

**Biggest Pain Point:** Partnership communication breakdown causing double-bookings, information silos, and relationship strain as they scale into new permanent venue (Feb-Mar 2025)

**Estimated Total Value:** ~6-10 hours reclaimed weekly, elimination of business-threatening coordination failures, relationship preservation

---

## Priority Opportunities

### 1. Unified Partnership Dashboard (Shared Everything) ðŸŽ¯
**Quadrant:** Quick Win (Low Effort, High Impact)

**Pain Point:**
Jenna and Derek have zero shared visibility. Physical wall calendar that Jenna photographs daily. Derek has "little notes on just pieces of paper everywhere" causing Jenna anxiety. Double-bookings occurring (Christmas party vs band booking). "Sometimes I don't know the event that's going on at the bar until I physically look at the calendar." Tuesday payroll = "scramble to get things from three different places." Information silos preventing effective partnership. "Is he going to move to Germany with you?" - relationship strain is real.

**Opportunity:**
Single shared dashboard (Google Sheets or Excel + shared folder) with tabs for: (1) Shared calendar (bar events + catering color-coded), (2) Bills due tracker, (3) Payroll information, (4) Revenue/expense summary, (5) Staff schedule. Both partners can update from anywhere. Auto-syncs. Printable for Jenna's physical preference. Jenna can still photograph calendar, but Derek sees it digitally instantly. Replaces scattered paper notes and calendar photos.

**Estimated Effort:**
- Time: 1-2 weeks
- Complexity: Low (Excel/Google Sheets template design + shared access setup)
- Dependencies: Choose platform (Google Sheets for real-time sync recommended), design layout, train both partners, migrate current calendar

**Estimated Value:**
- Time savings: 3-5 hours/week saved on coordination meetings and information gathering
- Revenue impact: Zero double-bookings = prevent revenue loss and customer disappointment
- Strategic value: **Business-critical** - current system will catastrophically fail at new venue scale, preserves partnership relationship

**ROI:** Immediate (prevents first double-booking in Week 1)

### 2. Automated Receipt Capture & Real-Time Expense Tracking ðŸŽ¯
**Quadrant:** Quick Win (Low Effort, High Impact)

**Pain Point:**
Jenna hates accounting "with a passion." Receipts "falling out everywhere" from purse. Manual Excel entry: date, expense account, pre-tax, tax, total. Takes "a couple hours" to catch up when neglected (procrastination is significant). Nightmares about disorganized receipts. 2 hours/week minimum when maintained. Delayed financial visibility = can't make real-time purchasing decisions ("I'll just run out and buy chicken at Superstore when I could have gotten it at Sobeys for cheaper"). Tried QuickBooks but couldn't separate food (no tax) vs non-food (taxed) items.

**Opportunity:**
Implement Expensify (iOS app, ~$5/month) with AI-powered receipt scanning. Snap photo instantly after purchase - auto-extracts date, vendor, amount. Custom categories for Nova Scotia tax rules (food/no tax vs supplies/taxed). Real-time expense dashboard visible to both Jenna & Derek. Export to accountant annually. Eliminates manual Excel entry completely. AI handles categorization - Jenna just reviews and confirms.

**Estimated Effort:**
- Time: 2-3 weeks
- Complexity: Low-Medium (Expensify setup + Nova Scotia tax category testing)
- Dependencies: Verify Expensify handles Nova Scotia tax rules, create custom categories, train Jenna on photo workflow, migrate existing Excel data

**Estimated Value:**
- Time savings: 2 hours/week = 104 hours annually
- Revenue impact: $2,000-3,000 annual value (time freed for revenue activities) + better purchasing decisions from real-time visibility
- Strategic value: Eliminates procrastination-causing pain point, reduces stress/nightmares, financial transparency for both partners

**ROI:** 2-3 month payback on subscription and setup time
